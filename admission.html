Admission Process

The procedure for admission into CIS is as follows :
1. Parents are required download the application form below
2. They are to submit the completed application form to the school with the following documents (photocopy and original for sighting):
 - Two (2) recent passport photographs
  - Birth certificate
 - Evidence of payment for application form and school fees(made in pupil's name)
 - Previous class results from previous school
3. The child is required to take an entrance examination.
4. Successful pupils are notified and offered admission.
